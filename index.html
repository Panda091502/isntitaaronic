    📖 How to Use This Site

    • Set Your Time – Enter your preferred work time using the input field and click Set. The longer your working time, the longer your break time.
    • Start Your Session – Click Work or Break to begin. The timer will auto-handle switching.
    • Use the Task List – Add tasks and click them to mark as done. ✅
    • Break Time Love – Enjoy cute motivational quotes on your breaks. 💜
    • Mobile Friendly – Use this site smoothly on phones and tablets!
